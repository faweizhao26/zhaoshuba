Privacy Policy

This page explains how Zhaoshuba handles analytics, advertising, cookies, local storage, and information related to external links.

Back to Home About Contact

1. What Information May Be Processed

Zhaoshuba does not provide user registration, login, or comment features, so we do not actively collect account-style information such as names or phone numbers.

When you visit the site, the analytics and advertising services used on the site may process technical data such as referral source, visit time, device and browser information, IP address, and page-view activity.

2. Analytics

This site uses Umami Analytics to understand traffic, traffic sources, and frequently visited content, so the site structure and curation can be improved over time.

Umami is a third-party analytics service. Please refer to its official materials for details about its implementation and data-handling rules.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

This site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, local storage, device identifiers, or similar technologies to show ads, measure ad performance, and prevent abuse.

You can learn more about how Google advertising technologies work here: Google Ads Technologies

4. Cookies and Local Storage

The site itself uses browser local storage for limited preference data, such as theme mode and language preference, in order to improve the browsing experience.

Third-party service providers may also use cookies or similar technologies. You can manage cookies in your browser settings, and where applicable, adjust ad personalization settings through the relevant third-party services.

5. External Links

This site contains many links to third-party websites. Once you click them, you leave Zhaoshuba and enter a site operated by someone else. Their privacy rules, cookie usage, and content practices are outside our control.
