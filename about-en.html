What This Site Does

Zhaoshuba is a curated directory for ebook resources, online reading sites, reading lists, academic resources, and reading tools. Its goal is to help visitors find useful reading-related websites faster.

The site focuses on navigation and curation. It does not directly host or distribute book files from third-party websites, and it does not guarantee that every external link will remain available over time.

How We Curate Links

  • We prioritize publicly accessible sites with clear information and reading relevance.
  • We prefer resources with search, reading, recommendation, or clear learning value.
  • Links may be added, updated, or removed based on quality, stability, and compliance risk.
  • We may decline or remove links that appear unclear, risky, or highly disputed.

Recommendations and Feedback

If you know a site that belongs here, feel free to send it by email and we will review it as soon as possible.

Your email should ideally include:
  • Site name and URL
  • Category
  • A one-line description
  • Tags or use cases (optional)
